OUR STORY

From a small garage in Nairobi to East Africa's premier destination for premium vehicles and expert service

Why We Exist

At The Bike Collector, we bridge the gap between enthusiasts and their dream machines. Our curated selection of superbikes, trikes, UTVs, and ATVs combines performance with reliability, backed by factory-trained technicians and genuine parts.

We don't just sell vehicles - we deliver exceptional riding experiences supported by Kenya's most knowledgeable team in high-performance machines.

Our Journey

2010

Humble Beginnings

Founded as a small motorcycle repair shop in Nairobi's industrial area, specializing in European superbike maintenance.

2014

First Import Batch

Successfully imported our first container of Ducati and BMW motorcycles, establishing direct relationships with European dealers.

2018

Expansion to UTVs/ATVs

Recognized market demand and expanded our portfolio to include utility and recreational vehicles from American manufacturers.

2023

Current Facility

Opened our 5,000 sq ft showroom and service center in Karen, featuring Kenya's only dyno testing facility for performance tuning.
